### Detailed Description

Meeting Copilot is your AI-powered assistant for Google Meet and Microsoft Teams meetings. Stay engaged and never miss important details with these powerful features:

**🎯 Private Audio Transcription**
- Click the "Listen" button to privately transcribe meeting audio
- Captures remote participants' voices without enabling in-meeting captions
- Keeps transcription private to you - not visible to other meeting attendees
- Uses OpenAI Whisper for accurate speech-to-text

**❓ Question Detection & AI Answers**
- Automatically detects questions asked during the meeting
- Requests concise, relevant AI answers based on meeting context
- Answers appear in the overlay for your review

**💬 Auto-Write to Chat**
- Insert AI-generated answers directly into the meeting chat
- Optional auto-send feature for hands-free operation
- Perfect for quick responses during busy meetings

**🔒 URL Gating**
- Only activates for the specific meeting link you configure
- Ensures the extension only assists in your intended meetings
- Set the meeting link via extension popup or web app integration

**🐛 Debug Mode**
- Live diagnostics panel to verify caption/chat detection
- Shows platform, captions found, chat input status, and gate match
- Helps troubleshoot on real meeting calls

**How It Works:**
1. Install the extension and configure your backend URL and AI credentials
2. Open a Google Meet or Microsoft Teams meeting
3. The overlay appears automatically when you're on a configured meeting
4. Click "Listen" for private transcription, or enable captions for Q&A
5. AI answers appear automatically when questions are detected

**Privacy & Security:**
- All transcription happens locally on your machine
- Audio is sent to your configured backend for processing
- No data is stored or shared with third parties
- You control which meetings the extension assists

**Requirements:**
- A running Meeting Copilot backend (self-hosted)
- OpenAI API key or compatible AI provider
- Google Meet or Microsoft Teams meeting

Perfect for professionals, students, and anyone who wants to stay fully engaged in meetings without missing critical information or questions.
